BohoPet Classic Chicken and Brown Rice


Dog Food


Product Description


Balancing your pet's diet is an art, perfected by DearPet's Chicken and Brown Rice Meal. Born from the love we had for Casper, each ingredient is selected to ensure your pet enjoys a vibrant and active life. This meal is a testament to our dedication, combining nutritious chicken, wholesome brown rice, and a mix of vegetables, all enriched with our nourishing bone broth. Choose this meal for a blend of care, nutrition, and taste that DearPet proudly stands for, making it perfect for active pets and caring pet parents alike.




What makes DearPet food different from other dog foods?


    DearPet foods are crafted with human-grade ingredients and cooked in bone broth, ensuring they are rich in natural nutrients. Unlike many other foods, we use no preservatives and our unique retort packaging keeps our food fresh without refrigeration.


How should I store DearPet food?


    Keep unopened pouches in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. There’s no need for refrigeration, making storage hassle-free.
